Surveillance Systems (CCTV and Intruder Alarms)

Dome Cameras

Ideal for indoor applications, these discreet cameras provide 360-degree coverage, making it difficult for potential intruders to detect which direction they are being monitored from.

Bullet Cameras

Perfect for outdoor use, these cameras are designed to be weatherproof and have a more extended viewing range.

PTZ Cameras (Pan-Tilt-Zoom)

These cameras can be remotely controlled to pan, tilt, and zoom, offering enhanced flexibility and the ability to focus on specific areas of interest.

Thermal Cameras

Utilizing infrared technology, our thermal cameras detect heat signatures, enabling surveillance even in low-light or no-light environments.

Wireless Cameras

Perfect for locations where wiring may not be feasible or as a temporary solution, wireless cameras offer flexibility in placement and can be easily relocated as needed.

Digital Video Recorders (DVR)

Comfort Home Innovations Limited also provide a wide range of Digital Video Recorders to store and manage surveillance footage efficiently:

Standalone DVR

Suitable for small-scale applications, standalone DVRs offer basic video recording and playback functionalities.

Network Video Recorders (NVR):

NVRs are designed for larger and more sophisticated setups, capable of handling multiple cameras and offering advanced features like remote access and analytics.

Hybrid DVRs

For clients transitioning from analog to IP-based systems, hybrid DVRs offer compatibility with both types of cameras, providing a cost-effective solution.

Antennae

To ensure reliable wireless connectivity, we also offer high-quality antennas that enhance the performance and range of our wireless CCTV systems. Our skilled technicians assess the surveillance environment and recommend the most suitable antennas for optimal signal transmission.
